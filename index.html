The numbers

Real impact
from one
cubic yard.

The poetry of Earth Funeral is backed by the science of ecology. Every figure here reflects what one cubic yard of living compost actually does in the real world.

400+ lbs CO₂ saved

Versus cremation, which releases approximately 400 lbs of CO₂. Human composting sequesters carbon rather than releasing it.

1B+ living microbes

One cubic yard of finished compost contains over one billion soil organisms — the foundation of all plant life and ecosystem health.

30× more fertile

Human compost is up to 30× more nutrient-dense than average garden soil, capable of restoring depleted land.

250+ plant species

A wildflower meadow seeded with this soil can support over 250 native plant species and the insects, birds, and mammals that depend on them.

3 mi pollination reach

Bees from a single thriving wildflower meadow pollinate across a 3-mile radius — fields, orchards, and wild landscapes alike.

∞ generations of renewal

The cycle carries no expiration. Every season, every generation, the soil gives again. A return with no final chapter.
